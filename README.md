# 📊 Telco Churn: Actionable Intelligence Project

## 🎯 Business Problem
The goal is to identify why customers are leaving and quantify the financial impact ($2.9M at risk).

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack
- **SQL (PostgreSQL):** Data cleaning and feature engineering.
- **Python (Scikit-Learn):** Machine Learning model (Random Forest) and Correlation Heatmaps.
- **Power BI:** Executive and Tactical Dashboards.

## 💡 Key Insights
1. **The Retention Cliff:** Customers are most likely to churn within the first 6 months.
2. **The Fiber Optic Paradox:** High-value Fiber Optic users churn at nearly 2x the rate of DSL users.
3. **Contractual Friction:** Month-to-month contracts account for the majority of revenue loss.

## 🚀 Recommendations
- Target Fiber Optic users with 1-year loyalty incentives.
- Automate outreach for customers with a Churn Risk Score > 80.
